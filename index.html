Beneath the Hollow Wind

Ambient music for sleep, focus, and meditation.

Beneath the Hollow Wind crafts immersive ambient soundscapes designed for long-form listening - 30-minute deep states, multi-hour study sessions, and full-night sleep companions. Drawing from natural elements like rain, fireplace, ocean tides, and cosmic drift, the music sits at the intersection of lo-fi, ambient, and Japanese atmospheric.

New release every two weeks from Tokyo, Japan.

About

An independent ambient music project based in Tokyo. All music is released weekly, focused on long-form listening for sleep, study, and meditation. Each album builds around a single mood and a specific natural element - rain, fireplace, stars, gardens.
